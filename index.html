FAQ & Policies

Do you sell the products you review?

No. We are strictly an independent review publication. We provide links to trusted retailers, and may earn an affiliate commission if you purchase through our links, but this does not affect our verdicts.

How long does a typical review take?

Our typical review cycle spans 3 to 4 weeks. This includes 1 week of lab burn-in and measurements, and 2-3 weeks of critical listening in various environments and setups by our team.

Editorial & Honesty Policy

Manufacturers do not read our reviews before they are published. We do not accept paid reviews. If a product sounds terrible, we will explicitly state it, regardless of the brand's reputation.
